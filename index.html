How To Use Dx2 Team Builder
Instructions:
  1. Select your first demon and the archetype you want them to have.
  2. Select their two inherited skills.
  3. Click the Brands option and assign the Brands for this demon.
  4. Repeat this process for the remaining 3 demons.
  5. Pick a liberator on the far right.
  6. Clicking 'Generate Link' will generate a unique URL to the build you just created.
  7. Clicking 'Reset' will reset the tool to its default state for you to build something else.
  8. For many 5* Demons you can Hover Over the demon/achetype image to get some additional details on how to use that demon.

    • Yes.

    • Maybe? If they come naturally on that demon always (Not an awaken or gacha skill) then yes they do. Otherwise they do not function at this time.
